Finally, an original retro RPG exclusively for Android!

BladeQuest is a new turn-based RPG currently in development. Modeled after the 8- and 16-bit classics you love, our goal is to return to basics with a strong emphasis on engaging story, charming characters, and evocative art and music that will immerse you in the nostalgia of that golden age.

BladeQuest is developed by Dapper Hat Games, a three-person team dedicated to recreating the fun of the classics and disregarding any fear of unoriginality.
